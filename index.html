1) Backyard Fungi

Click to learn more

Backyard Fungi is about creating a hyper-local food economy in the Twin Cities by empowering and educating normal people on how they can grow high-quality, local food in their own backyards. It was started by two urban dwellers in Minneapolis who were passionate about people having access to a sustainable food source. I helped by creating content on their website, and through developing their overall marketing strategy. Select the link above to learn more about Backyard Fungi and the impact it is making in Minneapolis, MN.
